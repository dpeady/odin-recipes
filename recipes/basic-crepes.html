Basic Crepes

Description

Simple yet delicious crepe batter which can be made in minutes for an easy breakfast or decidant dessert. It's made from ingredients that are almost guaranteed to be found at home, hence basic crepes! Simple and Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together flour and the eggs. Gradually add in the milk and water, sitting to combine. Add the salt and butter; beat until smooth.

  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.

  3. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side. Serve hot.
